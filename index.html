His Majesty, The King, Sir Harold Ravensleigh

Below is the brief description of His Majesty. This is to be updated, added to, and made much nicer looking in the future.


Roblox Description


Sir Harold Ravensleigh was born in 1848, and became King of The Crown Empire of Britain in 1871, at the age of 23. He appointed his brother, Sir Y Ravensleigh as second in command of The Empire as his first act as King. Since their reign begun, they have grown the Empire greatly and don't plan on stopping. They are currently reforming the Empire and making it more fair, just, and powerful.



Residence: The Palace of Ravensleigh
Age: 25
Full Name: Sir Harold Terrance Ravensleigh
